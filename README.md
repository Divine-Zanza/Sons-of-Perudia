# sons of Perudia
how to play!
sons of Perudia is broken into combat phases that follow in this order
1. Movement
2. Range
3. Melee
4. end of turn
the goal of the game is to build your list which is done randomly through using the attached code as a HTML file; and bring the first to destroy all enemy units

#Movement

1. to place your units on the board, you must place them within the first 3 squares on your border
2. in order to move during your move phase, you add your Movement modifier/ flight to the number rolled. if you roll a number and your modifier is negative, you do not move.

#Range 

1. Range is only done after movement but before Melee.

2. all Magic has a range of 10 squares

3. if a character is behind terrain it is considered outside of line if sight.

 #Melee

 1. in Melee you roll to hit, if you beat your opponent's armor score, you hit. then you roll damage. you total out the score on the dice, saving as many as possible. put those damage dice by the miniature to allocate damage.

if a character takes all the damage it can, it dies. remove it from the board.
